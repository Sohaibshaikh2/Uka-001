Education and careers

We are a specialty that is rewarding, varied and where you can make a huge impact. As your College we're by your side from pre-med to retirement and wherever you career as a paediatrician takes you. Learn more about how we can help your development.

RCPCH Progress+ is now live

Progress+ is our new two-level, run through specialty training programme. All new trainees and most current trainees will follow the Progress+ curriculum, which aims to be both flexible and fulfilling.

Go to our Progress+ hub for the full curriculum and syllabi, FAQs and resources for trainees, educational supervisors and clinical leads. You'll also find case studies on the 11 new training principles and the 11 curriculum domains.
